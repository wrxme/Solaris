notes:
	I'm working on solar system generation right now. In
	the future I'd like to add systems that can be binary
	or trinary star systems.
	Types of stars:
		- Class B (blue): 
			- large and hot
			- good for energy generation
			- lots of planets, fewer habitable ones
		- Class A (white):
			- bright and hot
			- good for research
		- Class G (yellow): 
			- like Sol
			- highest chance for habitable worlds
		- Class F (orange):
			- cooler than G
		- Class M (red dwarf): 
			- small and dim
			- planets close to the star
		- Red giant:
			- Large star
			- few planets, lots of asteroids
		- Blue giant:
			- rare
			- big
			- lots of planets
		- White dwarf:
			- small
			- planets have lots of minerals
		- Black hole
		- Neutron star
		- pulsar
